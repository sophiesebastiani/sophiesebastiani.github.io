Orwellian Industries: The Simulator Spring 2022

This is the day in the life of Orwellian Industries Employee #293915. You're a chill dude who just wants to play a chill game on your five-minute daily break but the ai manager keeps getting on your dang nerves!>:( Ignore the system messages as fast as you can so you can get back to avoiding virtual falling office supplies and slurping down delicious Monsters (mmm)!

Made in Unity 2D with Jelen Alysia. I thought it would be interesting to create a meta-layer first between the player and the simulated player and then again the simulated reality of the Employee and the reality of someone likely playing this on a computer similar to the laptop in the game which I created in Photoshop using clipart (even the chips were a separate layer lol) as well as the main character that moves back and forth across the screen in Office Simulator.
