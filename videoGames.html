Video Games:

A Day Like Any Other Spring 2023

Unity 3D game made with Jeeyeon Barnes in Digital Worlds for Gaming. "On a day like any other, you recieve a letter." We were inspired by the Truman Show and the Stanley Parable to conceive of a world encapsulated within a perfectly identical suburb in which our main character lives, content with his mundane life with no connection to the real world until the illusion is broken. In creating the game's point of view, the fixed angles of the cameras create a feeling of surveillance that combined with the uncanny perfection of the outside world is already strange. This demo serves as the first part of a larger game, which begins after the main camera switches from the fixed perspectives to a third-person follow cam, signifying that the main character has finally taken control of his life and is ready for the reality-breaking journey ahead of him.


Project Terra Spring 2023

Unity 3D game made with Janavi Padala in Digital Worlds for Gaming. Project Terra is a game where you land in a what seems to be a quarry inside a mysterious world, with only the company of your loyal robot companion 2-T0R. Once you climb out, you decide to navigate to the only landmark in sight, an imposing building in the middle of a dessert which holds a terrifying secret. My focus on the project was shaping the landscape and architecture of this world through 3D modeling, asset integration and lighting within the Unity 3D game engine.

Orwellian Industries: The Simulator Spring 2022

WARNING: I'm working on fixing this soon but the sound is exceedingly loud on this so turn down the volume your individual computer before you hit play please!

This is the day in the life of Orwellian Industries Employee #293915. You're a chill dude who just wants to play a chill game on your five-minute daily break but the a.i. manager keeps getting on your dang nerves!>:( Ignore the system messages as fast as you can so you can get back to avoiding virtual falling office supplies and slurping down delicious Monsters (mmm)!

Made in Unity 2D with Jelen Alysia. I thought it would be interesting to create a meta-layer first between the player and the simulated player and then again with the simulated reality of the Employee character (drawing inspiration from the Stanley Parable game) and the reality of a user *likely* playing this on a computer similar to the laptop in the game. I created this background layer, and all of the ui elements (such as the Orwellian Industries title screen, system messages, game instructions, etc.) in Photoshop, as well as the 2D character that moves back and forth across the screen in Office Simulator.

Play the game here! For best results play on a 1920 x 1080 display. For mac users, parts of the ui aren't scaled correctly sorry!:(
