    • A Day Like Any Other Spring 2023

    "On a day like any other, you recieve a letter." A Day Like Any Other is a 3D puzzle and narrative-based game where our main character lives in an idyllic and identical suburban neighborhood, content with his mundane life until the monotony of his everyday routine is interrupted with the arrival of a strange letter from a long-time pen pal.

    This demo serves as the first part of a *theoretically* much larger game that explores themes of surveillance (visualized through fixed camera angles that eventually switch to a 3rd person follow cam) and identity, pulling inspiration from the Stanley Parable and Truman Show. Unity 3D game made with Jeeyeon Barnes in Digital Worlds for Gaming at Wellesley College.


    Orwellian Industries: The Simulator Spring 2022

    This is the day in the life of Orwellian Industries Employee #293915. You're a regular dude who just wants to chill and play a relaxing game on your five-minute daily break but the AI manager keeps getting on your dang nerves! Ignore the system messages as fast as you can so you can get back to avoiding virtual falling office supplies and slurping down delicious Monsters (yum)!

    I thought it would be interesting to create a meta-layer first between the player and the simulated player and then again with the simulated reality of the Employee character and the reality of a user *likely* playing this on a laptop similar to the one in the game. I created the background layer of an office worker's dirty desk (napkins, chip crumbs, empty cans of coke and Monster of course, etc.) and all of the ui elements (such as the Orwellian Industries title screen, system messages, game instructions, etc.), as well as the 2D character that moves back and forth across the 'Office Simulator' game in Photoshop. Made in Unity 2D in Intro to Game Design at Wellesley College.

    Project Terra Spring 2023

    In Project Terra, you land in the quarry of a mysterious world, with only the company of your loyal robot companion 2-T0R to aid you in your exploration. Once you complete the quarry tutorial stage, you decide to navigate to the only landmark in sight, an imposing museum in the middle of a dessert which holds a terrifying secret. My focus on the project was shaping the landscape and architecture of this world through 3D modeling, asset integration and lighting within the Unity 3D game engine. Unity 3D game made with Janavi Padala in Digital Worlds for Gaming at Wellesley College.
