The Myth of the Self-Regulatory Machine Spring 2023

Documentary project for Advanced Video Production at Wellesley College. I was drawn to this project, particularly the regulatory aspect, because of the magnitude of the issues surrounding 3d printing and artificial intelligence, the game of hot potato the Supreme Court is playing with Congress over this problem, and the difficulties the 1st Amendment (+ others) poses to passing any legislation at all. I wish I could have spent an entire year creating this documentary and I hope to uptdate it in the future; but, the timing of this film is very important to me with the information it shares about large language models (LLM) and their flaws ripe for regulatory action. Acting as director, cameraman + crew, and editor this time was incredibly challenging but ultimately rewarding as it allowed complete control for me to tighten this vast observation into a cohesive narrative and pose some interesting questions about the freedom of internet usage in the future along the way.
