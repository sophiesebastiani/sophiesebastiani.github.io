    • A Day Like Any Other Spring 2023

    "On a day like any other, you recieve a letter." Inspired by the Truman Show and the Stanley Parable, A Day Like Any Other is a virtual world encapsulated within the perfectly identical suburb our main character lives in, content with his mundane life until the monotony of his everyday routine is interrupted with the arrival of a strange letter from a long-time pen pal.

    In creating the game's point of view, the fixed angles of the cameras allow a perspective of surveillance that, combined with the uncanny perfection of the outside world, creates an uncanny playing experience.

    This demo serves as the first part of a *theoretically* much larger game, which begins after the main camera switches from the fixed perspectives to a third-person follow cam, signifying that the main character has finally taken control of his life and is ready for the reality-breaking journey ahead of him. Unity 3D game made with Jeeyeon Barnes in Digital Worlds for Gaming at Wellesley College.
