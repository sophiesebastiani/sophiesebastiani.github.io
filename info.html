    Sophie Sebastiani (b. Alabama, 2000) works with photo, video, interactive and other digital media to explore technological and economic development in the US and record its effects on public action, interpersonal relationships and the environment.

    She received a B.A. in Economics and Media Arts and Sciences from Wellesley College (Wellesley, MA) in 2023. She has also curated, installed and mounted exhibits for Jewett Art Center Galleries, and assisted working artists with painting and photography projects. See CV below.
