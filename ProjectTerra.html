Project Terra Spring 2023

Unity 3D game made with Janavi Padala in Digital Worlds for Gaming. in Project Terra, you land in the quarry of a mysterious world, with only the company of your loyal robot companion 2-T0R in your exploration. Once you complete the quarry tutorial stage, you decide to navigate to the only landmark in sight, an imposing museum in the middle of a dessert which holds a terrifying secret. My focus on the project was shaping the landscape and architecture of this world through 3D modeling, asset integration and lighting within the Unity 3D game engine.
