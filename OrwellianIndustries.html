    • Orwellian Industries: The Simulator Spring 2022

    This is the day in the life of Orwellian Industries Employee #293915. You're a regular dude who just wants to chill and play a relaxing game on your five-minute daily break but the AI manager keeps getting on your dang nerves! Ignore the system messages as fast as you can so you can get back to avoiding virtual falling office supplies and slurping down delicious Monsters (yum)! Made in Unity 2D in Intro to Game Design at Wellesley College.
