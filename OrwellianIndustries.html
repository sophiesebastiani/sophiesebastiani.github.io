Orwellian Industries: The Simulator Spring 2022

This is the day in the life of Orwellian Industries Employee #293915. You're a regular dude who just wants to chill and play a relaxing game on your five-minute daily break but the a.i. manager keeps getting on your dang nerves! >:( Ignore the system messages as fast as you can so you can get back to avoiding virtual falling office supplies and slurping down delicious Monsters (yum)!

Made in Unity 2D. I thought it would be interesting to create a meta-layer first between the player and the simulated player and then again with the simulated reality of the Employee character and the reality of a user *likely* playing this on a laptop similar to the one in the game. I created the background layer of an office worker's dirty desk (napkins, chip crumbs, empty cans of coke and Monster of course, etc.) and all of the ui elements (such as the Orwellian Industries title screen, system messages, game instructions, etc.), as well as the 2D character that moves back and forth across the 'Office Simulator' game in Photoshop.
