Hi! My name is Sophie Sebastiani and I graduated with cum laude honors from Wellesley College in May 2023, earning my B.A. in Economics and Media Arts and Sciences (an multidisciplinary major that unites the fields of computer science and studio art).

I work primarily with photo, video, interactive and other digital+visual media to explore technological and economic development while challenging the model of free market capitalism and the exponential growth it promises.

With a new wave of the technological revolution cresting over us and the reality of a.i. automating online processes and disrupting global labor hierarchies, I am exploring how to design online experiences that make digital spaces more equitable in terms of user rights and collective ownership.
