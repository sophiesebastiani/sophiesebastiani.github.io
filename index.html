My name is Sophie Sebastiani and I graduated with cum laude honors from Wellesley College in May 2023, earning my B.A. in Economics and Media Arts and Sciences (an multidisciplinary major that unites the fields of computer science and studio art).

I work with photo, video, interactive and other visual media to explore technological and economic development histories and futures and the borders between reality and digital manipulation. Given my personal investment in the economic conditions and developing landscape of my southern Alabama hometown, I am particularly interested in conducting research with relevant applications in promoting increased standards of living in the United States.
