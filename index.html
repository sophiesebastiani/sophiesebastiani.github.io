Hi! My name is Sophie Sebastiani and I graduated with cum laude honors from Wellesley College in May 2023, earning my B.A. in Economics and Media Arts and Sciences (an multidisciplinary major that unites the fields of computer science and studio art).

As a designer, I create products that emphasize user accessibility and focus on addressing community needs. As an artist, I work primarily with photo, video, and other digital media to explore technological and economic development while challenging the model of free market capitalism and the exponential growth it promises.

With a new wave of the technological revolution cresting over us and the reality of a.i. automating online processes and disrupting global labor hierarchies, I am also interested in exploring how to design online experiences that make digital spaces more equitable in terms of user rights and collective ownership.
