Flashes: a Stop Motion Visualizer Fall 2021

Photographed and Edited for Intro to 2D Design at Wellesley College

This assignment was all about movement, audio, and finding visual rythym. I chose to overlay Japanese Breakfast's Posing in Bondage (2021) with visuals due to its fast bpm which allowed for the rapid cuts necessitated by the many frames I used, and also because the song really speaks to a basic human need and desire for companionship, a complicated sense of yearning that I attempted to convey in less than 2 minutes':). I used Adobe AfterEffects to create animations paired with digital photographs I took of my friends and edited into a sequence reminiscent of stop motion filmography.
