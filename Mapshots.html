Mapshots: Semester UI/UX Mobile Design Project Spring 2023

Human Computer Interaction semester project. Mapshots is a community-focused mobile application with the goal of creating a spatial memory of the Wellesley College campus and its past, present, and future student body, their traditions, and record their four years at Wellesley. Designed by Sophie Sebastiani ('23), Euphy Liu ('23), Lyra Kalajian('24), Ariel Traver ('23), and Diana Padrón('24) in Spring of 2023. Visit our official Mapshots site for an in-depth overview of our ideation, prototyping and testing processes. Below is a demo of our final high-fidelity Figma prototype. Click on the phone to interact and scroll to navigate around the map!
