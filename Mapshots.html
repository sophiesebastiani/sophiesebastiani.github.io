Mapshots CS 220 semester project - Spring 2023

Mapshots is a community-focused mobile application with the goal of creating a spatial memory of the Wellesley College designed by Sophie Sebastiani, Euphy Liu, Lyra Kalajian, Ariel Traver, and Diana Padrón in Spring 2023.

Below is a walkthrough of our Figma low-fidelity wireframe prototype. We are currently in the stage of developing a high-fidelity profile, utilizing feedback from our first round of usability testing.
